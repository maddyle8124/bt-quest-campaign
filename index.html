Our Approach

How we design these campaigns

Principle 01

Rewards are earned, not handed out

Quest bundles require real product use, trading activity, or holding behavior, so budget is tied to the work being paid for.

Principle 02

Long-bias by design

Hold and accumulation quests carry more weight than rapid-cycle trades, so incentives support staying in position rather than churning.

Principle 03

Fair allocation

Slot allocation is pipeline-based with per-pack caps and time windows, with no insider preference or off-platform side deals.

Principle 04

No wash trading. No coordinated accounts

The goal is real participant trading and real market candles, with anti-bot screening built into daily operations.

Next Steps

Four questions to scope your campaign

Question 01

What catalysts are landing during the campaign window? Listings, releases, partnership announcements, and token unlocks set the timeline tempo.

Question 02

What is the utility of your native token, and what behaviors should quests reinforce? Holding, trading, staking, product use, or governance.

Question 03

What budget range should we design around? Stablecoin or native token, and if native, what is the release schedule?

Question 04

What is the primary KPI for this campaign? Volume, holders, product engagement, community size, or a weighted mix.
